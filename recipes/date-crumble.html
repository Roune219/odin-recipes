Date Crumble

Cook's Note

To make your own dried lime zest: zest your limes with a small-mesh grater and either leave the zest
to dry naturally or put it in a 200 degree F (95 degree C) oven for an hour or two, until it hardens up,
crush it with a mortar and pestle until it's small enough to use as a spice.

Directions

Combine the lime zest, black pepper, and salt in an jar. Shake to mix. Use in recipes where you would use lemon pepper.
